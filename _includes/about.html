A brief summary of my career...

  • 1995

    The Start

    My first violin lesson was hard, but fun.

  • 2000-2008

    Pre-University

    I studied piano at the Royal Conservatory of Music, violin at the Young Artists' Performance Academy (now called the Phil and Eli Taylor Academy), and also joined the Toronto Symphony Youth Orchestra.

  • June 2017

    Finished!

    I graduated with a Bachelor of Music from McGill, and a Master of Music in Performance Violin from the University of Toronto.

  • November 2018

    The Next Chapter

    I began my work at Fieldstone King's College School. I am also now a part of Fieldstone Day School. I teach music classes and include vocal, Orff, and violin across all grades, K to 12. I also perform all across Ontario!
